About Alf!

First AWS Cloud Club in the country

The AWS Cloud Club at PUP Manila aims to foster a community of cloud enthusiasts, offering resources, workshops, and events that will help members develop their skills and knowledge in cloud computing. This initiative aligns perfectly with the ever-evolving landscape of technology and the increasing demand for cloud expertise in various industries. We encourage all students interested in cloud computing, whether you're just starting or have some experience, to get involved with the AWS Cloud Club. It's a chance to learn, collaborate, and explore the limitless possibilities that the cloud can offer.
